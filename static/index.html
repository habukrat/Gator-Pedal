The Details

The Problem

The problem is that music, especially produced by guitars, has not kept up with technology over the last couple of decades. The technology available to guitar players requires a lot of different expensive equipment. There is a lack of internet connectivity and support for sharing creative ideas among members of the community.

The Solution

Our product solves the problem by offering an all-in-one alternative that is still compatible with other equipment. We make the technology easy to use by allowing users to create profiles and save settings on the pedal for quick access. The Pelican Pedal provides those connected features in order to create a community for users to share their profiles and settings with other players.

The Features

  • Wireless
  • Multiple Mappable Effects
  • Looper
  • Recording, Saving, and Sharing Audio
  • MIDI Support
  • Tilt Effects
  • Sharable User Profiles and Settings
